Quick Answers

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship physical hardware and dry goods internationally. Live cultures (SCOBYs) are limited to the US and Canada due to transit times.

My SCOBY sank to the bottom. Is it dead?

No! It's completely normal for a SCOBY to sink, float in the middle, or stay at the top. A new pellicle will eventually form across the surface of the liquid.

How do I cancel my tea subscription?

You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. There are no cancellation fees.
