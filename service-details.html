How Continuous Brewing Works

1

The Initial Setup

Brew a concentrated sweet tea, add it to the vessel with water, and gently place the monster SCOBY and starter liquid on top. Wait 10-14 days.

2

Harvesting

Once the brew reaches your desired tartness (usually pH 3.0), open the spigot and draw off up to 30% of the liquid for drinking or second fermentation.

3

Topping Up

Replace the harvested liquid with fresh sweet tea. Because the existing culture is so established, the new batch will be ready in just 2-4 days!
